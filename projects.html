Time Since Death Estimation - 2023

Investigated the field of time since death estimation using a diffusion-based approach, specifically focusing on potassium concentration changes in the vitreous humor. Developed a Python program to model and visualize the relationship between potassium reduction and the time since death. My work contributes to more precise post-mortem forensic analysis.
